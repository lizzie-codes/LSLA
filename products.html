  • Short Term Loan
    • It is available to all LSLA members with contribution of at least Php 1,000.00.
    • Maximum loanable amount is up to Php 18,000.00 depending on the level of the member.
    • With an add-on rate of 1%.
    • Payable for 1 month only.
  • Emergency Loan
    • Available to all LSLA members with at least 6 months membership tenure.
    • Must have at least Php 3,000.00 total capital contribution.
    • Maximum loanable amount is up to Php 100,000.00 depending on the level of the member.
    • With an add-on rate of 8.31% or 15% effective rate per annum.
  • Educational Loan
    • Available to all LSLA members with at least 12 months membership tenure.
    • Must have at least Php 5,000.00 total capital contribution.
    • Maximum loanable amount is up to Php 150,000.00 depending on the tenure of the membership.
    • With an add-on rate of 8.31% or 15% effective rate per annum
  • Multi-purpose Loan
    • Available to all LSLA members with at least 12 months membership tenure.
    • Must have at least Php 5,000.00 total capital contribution.
    • Maximum loanable amount is up to Php 150,000.00 depending on the tenure of the membership.
    • With an add-on rate of 8.31% or 15% effective rate per annum.
  • Long Term Loan
    • Available to all LSLA members with at least 12 months membership tenure.
    • Must have at least Php 20,000.00 total capital contribution.
    • Maximum loanable amount is up to Php 1,500,000.00.
    • With an add-on rate of 8.31% or 15% effective rate per annum.
    • Maximum term is up to 60 months.
